# Research: Pitfalls

## Pitfall: Copyright Drift

**Warning signs:** copied lesson text, untracked external examples, audio files without provenance.

**Prevention:** every external resource gets owner, URL, license status, and allowed-use notes before use.

**Phase:** Phase 1 and every content phase.

## Pitfall: Kanji Familiarity Becomes Misleading

**Warning signs:** entries imply same meaning or pronunciation just because characters match.

**Prevention:** include caution fields for false friends, tone differences, Taiwan usage, and cases where Japanese meaning diverges.

**Phase:** Phase 2.

## Pitfall: Generic Language App Feel

**Warning signs:** bland flashcards, no Taiwan travel context, no reason to continue reading.

**Prevention:** each lesson needs a learner outcome, a travel or curiosity hook, and review prompts.

**Phase:** Phase 2 and Phase 3.

## Pitfall: Overbuilding Accounts Before Value

**Warning signs:** auth, profiles, cloud sync, and dashboards appearing before content is useful.

**Prevention:** v1 uses public content and local practice only.

**Phase:** Phase 3.

## Pitfall: Mobile Text Overlap

**Warning signs:** long Japanese explanations squeezed into cards or buttons.

**Prevention:** stable responsive layouts, browser screenshots, and content length checks before shipping UI.

**Phase:** Phase 1 onward.
