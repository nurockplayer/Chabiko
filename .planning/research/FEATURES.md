# Research: Features

## Table Stakes

- Beginner learning path with a visible progression.
- Mobile-friendly lesson pages.
- Traditional Chinese, pinyin, and Japanese explanations.
- Phrasebook organized by travel scenario.
- Vocabulary pages searchable or filterable by category.
- Practice interactions for recall.
- Clear external resource attribution.

## Differentiators

- Mandarin/Japanese on-yomi-similar vocabulary as a first-class learning surface.
- "Looks familiar, sounds different" warnings for false friends and pronunciation traps.
- Taiwan travel framing instead of generic Mandarin study.
- Fun, readable lesson voice that encourages continuation.

## Anti-Features

- A giant dictionary UI without guidance.
- Copy-heavy textbook pages with no interaction.
- Audio/video dependency before content quality is proven.
- Login wall for basic learning.
- Machine-generated examples without review.

## V1 Content Targets

- 10 beginner lessons.
- 50 on-yomi-similar vocabulary entries.
- 30 travel phrases across 6 scenarios.
- 3 lightweight practice modes.
- Resource page with license status for every linked resource.
