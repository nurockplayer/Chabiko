## Goal

Draft the first 10 beginner lessons for Japanese speakers learning Mandarin for Taiwan travel.

## Scope

- Define the lesson sequence from absolute beginner to travel basics.
- Write at least 10 short lessons.
- Each lesson needs Japanese hook, learner outcome, Traditional Chinese examples, pinyin, Japanese explanation, and review prompts.
- Each lesson follows the Chabiko lesson loop: hook, can-do goal, core sentence, chunk breakdown, kanji bridge, sound focus, mini practice, travel task, and review hook.
- Flag Taiwan-specific wording where relevant.

## Out of scope

- Audio recording.
- Full grammar reference.
- Production UI design.

## Acceptance criteria

- [ ] 10 lesson files exist in the structured content format.
- [ ] Every lesson has learner outcome, hook, examples, pinyin, Japanese explanation, and review prompts.
- [ ] Every core lesson follows the Chabiko lesson loop.
- [ ] Each lesson includes a concrete Taiwan travel task or Travel Quest connection.
- [ ] Content review metadata is present for every lesson.
- [ ] Lessons avoid copying third-party copyrighted text.
