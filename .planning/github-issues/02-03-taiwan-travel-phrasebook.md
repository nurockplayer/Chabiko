## Goal

Create the v1 Taiwan travel phrasebook dataset.

## Scope

- Add at least 30 travel phrases across airport, transport, food, shopping, hotel, and emergency scenarios.
- Include simple dialog examples.
- Include Traditional Chinese, pinyin, Japanese explanation, and scenario metadata.
- Link phrases to related vocabulary where known.

## Out of scope

- Full travel guide content.
- Emergency/legal advice beyond basic phrase support.
- Audio files.

## Acceptance criteria

- [ ] 30 phrase entries exist across six scenarios.
- [ ] Each scenario has at least one simple dialog.
- [ ] Every phrase has Traditional Chinese, pinyin, Japanese explanation, and source/review metadata.
- [ ] Taiwan travel usage is explicit where relevant.
