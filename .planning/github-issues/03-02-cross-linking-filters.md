## Goal

Connect lessons, vocabulary, and travel phrases so learners can explore by context.

## Scope

- Add filters or navigation for vocabulary category, caution flags, and travel context.
- Link travel phrases to related vocabulary.
- Link vocabulary back to relevant lessons or scenarios.
- Keep interactions usable on mobile.

## Out of scope

- Full search indexing service.
- User bookmarks or saved lists.

## Acceptance criteria

- [ ] Learner can filter or navigate vocabulary by category, caution, and travel context.
- [ ] Learner can move from travel phrase to related vocabulary and lesson context.
- [ ] False-friend/caution metadata is visible in the UI.
- [ ] Navigation works without login or backend state.
